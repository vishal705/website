About Us

At Spice Factory, we believe the best dishes are the ones that remind you of childhood. A portion of great food should make you feel like you are wrapped in a warm blanket.

While we prepare our food fast, we develop the flavor slowly. Our dough rises over the night, and our meat is always marinated. That's why every chicken tender and every bun taste like heaven.

Forget about the daily stress and come relax with us. After tasting any of our menu items, you will never want to leave!
